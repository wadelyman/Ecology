Welcome to the Document Variable Control tool! This allows you to make wholesale changes to values that tend to vary within standard Ampion documents. For example, you have an introductory letter that you regularly send to major clients, but who you're sending it to and other key metadata regularly changes; with Document Variable Control you can store a template of the document with all of the varying terms simply defined by being surrounded by {curly brackets}. Then when you generate a copy of that template, and open Variable Control you can update every variable instantly at the click of a button.
