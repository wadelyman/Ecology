Control Page

In order to comply with information security standards, we have to define every document we make in terms of information classification. As such, every document must be labelled with one of the following four designations: Public, Internal, Confidential, and Secret. This toolset helps Ampionites comply while their producing documents by allowing them to check for compliance, and automatically add the appropriate designations at the click of a button.

See if your current document contains the nessesary information classification components.
